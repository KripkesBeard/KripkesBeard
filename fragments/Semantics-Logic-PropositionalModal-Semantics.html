evaluateAtIn :: PropositionalModalFormula -> World -> KripkeModel -> Bool #

Evaluates the truth of a formula at a world according to a Kripke model.

Basically a structural recursion over the logical form of the formula. Necessity is evaluated by mapping the evaluation of the formula at each world accessible by the input world, and then folding over that set of booleans with (&&), similarly for possibility but with (||).
